Guest Instructors

Angelika and Oliver Kromer

Some of you may have met Angelika & Oliver Kromer & family before? They were Guest Instructors at CLOG ON in Anglesea, Victoria in November 2022. Such an energetic, friendly, clogging-loving family!

Angelika has taught at several ECTA Clogging Conventions, Jamborees and Workshops in Europe and also at the Fontana Clogging Jamboree in the US. In 1994, she was one of the founders of the Clogging Council in ECTA (European Caller and Teacher Association) in 1994.

Oliver Kromer - known as Olli, has been dancing for 22 years and teaching for 16 years. Together with his mother Angelika, and his younger brother Jan - he teaches the Barn Stompers e.V in Kirchheim am Neckar, Germany. He has taught at several national conventions and workshops in Germany as well as internationally at the City Slick Stomp and Fontana Clogging Convention.
